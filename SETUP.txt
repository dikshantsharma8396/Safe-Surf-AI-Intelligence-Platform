Welcome to the Safe-Surf AI ecosystem. Follow these steps 
to activate your real-time phishing protection.

--- PART 1: BROWSER EXTENSION SETUP ---

1. Open your Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave).
2. Type 'chrome://extensions/' in the address bar and hit Enter.
3. In the top right corner, toggle 'Developer mode' to ON.
4. Click the 'Load unpacked' button.
5. Select the 'Safe-Surf_Extension' folder from this package.
6. Look for the Safe-Surf icon in your toolbar and 'Pin' it for quick access.

--- PART 2: BACKEND CONNECTIVITY ---

- INTERNAL USERS: Ensure the Lead Architect has the local Flask 
  server running at http://127.0.0.1:5000.
- EXTERNAL USERS: The extension is pre-configured to point to 
  the Safe-Surf Cloud Intelligence Core. No action required.

--- PART 3: HOW TO USE ---

1. SCANNING: Click the Safe-Surf icon to open the popup. 
   Paste any URL and click 'Analyze'.
2. PASSIVE PROTECTION: As you browse, the AI silently 
   checks every site. If a threat (like a Punycode Spoof) 
   is detected, a warning will be triggered.
3. AUDIT REPORTS: After a scan, click 'Download Forensic Audit' 
   to get a detailed PDF breakdown of the URL's DNA.

----------------------------------------------------------
SECURITY ALERT: This AI detects Punycode/Homograph attacks 
(e.g., apple.com spoofs) that are invisible to the human eye. 
Always check the 'Homograph Detection' status in your reports.
